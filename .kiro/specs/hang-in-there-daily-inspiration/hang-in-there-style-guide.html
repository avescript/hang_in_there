Buttons & Actions

All buttons use pill-shaped borders to feel approachable and human. Interactions use a material-style ripple on click, spring easing on hover, and a gentle icon wiggle ‚Äî buttons should feel alive and tactile. The dark walnut secondary CTA grounds the palette with warmth and depth. Never use sharp rectangular buttons; they feel corporate and cold.

Voice & Tone

Every word on the platform should feel like it was written by a thoughtful human friend ‚Äî not a wellness app, not a motivational poster, not a corporate HR department. Warm. Precise. Honest. Unadorned.

Write like this

"Today's story is here. It's a good one."

"She didn't set out to save the reef. She just couldn't watch it die."

"A celebration, not a streak to break."

"No ads. No algorithms. No manipulation."

Never write like this

"YOU'VE GOT THIS! üöÄ Unlock your full potential TODAY!"

"Inspiring stories curated by AI just for you!"

"Don't break your streak ‚Äî keep your momentum going!"

"Join millions of happy users changing the world!"

üåø

Warm, not saccharine

Acknowledge that the world is hard. Don't deny it. Stories earn their hope ‚Äî they don't demand it.

üîç

Precise, not vague

Name the person. Name the place. Name the outcome. Verified specifics are more moving than abstract inspiration.

ü§ù

Companion, not coach

The platform sits beside you ‚Äî never above you. It offers a story; it never tells you what to feel about it.
