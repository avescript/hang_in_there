**Overview**

This document defines five primary user personas for the Hang In There platform. Each persona is grounded in the PRD's target audience research ‚Äî professionals experiencing burnout, values-driven adults seeking authentic hope, educators, community builders, and purpose-seeking young professionals. Together, they represent the range of real people who will find genuine value in one verified, beautifully told story per day.

Personas are organized from highest product-fit urgency to broadest growth opportunity. Each includes at-a-glance demographics, motivations, pain points, media behavior, app use patterns, feature priorities, and a design/tone consideration for the product team.
